About Us

Welcome to the world of Dior Sauvage, where elegance meets the wild. Inspired by wide-open spaces and the raw beauty of the nature, Dior Sauvage is a fragrance that embodies strength, sophistication, and freedom. Launched in 2015 and crafted by the renowned perfumer Fran√ßois Demachy, Dior Sauvage was designed to capture the essence of the untamed wilderness, blending fresh, spicy, and woody notes to create a scent that is both powerful and refined.





At Dior Sauvage, we believe in the power of nature and the importance os authenticaly. Our fragrance is a tribute to the raw, rugged landscapes that inspire us, and we are committed to using high-quality, natural ingredients in our formulations. Each bottle of Dior Sauvage is a testament to our dedication to excellence and our passion for creating timeless, unforgettable scents.





Our product line includes the Sauvage Eau de Toilette, a fresh and spicy fragrance with notes of bergamot, Sichuan pepper, and ambroxan; the Sauvage Eau de Parfum, a deeper, more intense version of the original, featuring notes of vanilla, star anise, and nutmeg; and the Sauvage Parfum, the most concentrated form of the fragrance, with rich notes of sandalwood, tonka bean, and frankincense.





We are dedicated to sustainability and ethical practices. Form sourcing our ingredients to our packaging, we strive to minimize our environmental impact and support fair trade practices. Dior Sauvage is more than just a fragrance; it is a symbol of our respect for nature and our commitment to creating a better world.
