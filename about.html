About Me

Hello! I'm Emmanuel Mojeca, a passionate photographer and videographer with a knack for capturing the essence of every moment. My journey into photography and videography began as a simple hobby and transformed into a lifelong love for storytelling through visuals. Whether it’s landscapes, portraits, or street photography and also filmmaking, I strive to create timeless memories with every click of my camera.

Based in a city full of inspiration, I have worked with numerous clients, bringing their visions to life. When I’m not behind the lens, you can find me exploring new places, seeking fresh perspectives, and perfecting my craft.
